For example, we may record notable information provided in YouTube comments, like feedback and suggestions, for the purposes of improving our content in the future. In this, we may also record other, unrelated information provided to us, like your YouTube account handle and any other text written in the comment.

We will not knowingly record any information that is personally identifiable, and if information is determined to be personally identifiable at any time, it will be immediately and permanently deleted. We will never knowingly record information (of any nature) about anyone under the age of 13. However, information we delete may still be accessible if a third party has copied the text or saved an image of the comment. In these cases, Quest Media is not able to take any action, nor will it be held responsible.
